Candidate Agreement

JobExhibition.com

This Candidate Agreement outlines the terms and conditions governing the use of JobExhibition.com by job seekers and candidates who create profiles, upload resumes, or apply for job opportunities through the platform.

By registering as a candidate on JobExhibition.com, you agree to comply with the terms described in this agreement.

1. Candidate Account Registration
  • Provide accurate and complete personal information.
  • Maintain the confidentiality of your login credentials.
  • Update profile information when necessary.
  • Use the platform only for legitimate job search purposes.

JobExhibition.com reserves the right to suspend accounts containing false or misleading information.

2. Profile Information and Resume Upload
  • All information provided in the profile or resume is accurate and truthful.
  • Uploaded documents belong to the candidate.
  • Fake qualifications, work experience, or certifications are not permitted.
  • Providing false information may result in account suspension or removal.
3. Profile Visibility
  • Profiles may become visible to recruiters and consultancies using the platform.
  • Recruiters may view or download resumes depending on their subscription access.
  • Candidate information may be used by employers to evaluate job applications.
  • Candidates can update or modify their profile details at any time.
4. Job Applications
  • JobExhibition.com does not guarantee job interviews or employment.
  • Recruiters independently evaluate job applications.
  • Selection decisions are made solely by employers.
  • JobExhibition.com only provides the technology platform connecting candidates with recruiters.
5. Communication with Recruiters
  • Respond professionally to recruitment inquiries.
  • Avoid sending spam or irrelevant messages.
  • Respect recruiter communication guidelines.
6. Prohibited Activities
  • Create multiple fake accounts.
  • Upload fraudulent resumes or documents.
  • Impersonate other individuals.
  • Use the platform for illegal activities.
  • Attempt to access unauthorized platform data.
7. Data Privacy and Security
  • Profile and resume information may be shared with recruiters for hiring purposes.
  • Certain information may be visible to employers searching for candidates.
  • JobExhibition.com takes reasonable measures to protect user data.
8. Fraud Awareness

Candidates should be cautious when interacting with recruiters.

  • JobExhibition.com does not charge candidates for job applications or placements.
  • If any recruiter asks for payment related to a job opportunity, candidates should report the incident immediately.
9. Account Suspension or Termination
  • Provide false information.
  • Violate platform policies.
  • Engage in fraudulent or abusive activities.
  • Misuse platform features.
10. Limitation of Liability
  • JobExhibition.com acts only as a technology platform connecting candidates and recruiters.
  • The platform does not guarantee employment opportunities.
  • The platform does not guarantee accuracy of job listings.
  • Recruiter behaviour or hiring decisions are outside platform control.
11. Changes to the Agreement

JobExhibition.com may update this Candidate Agreement periodically. Users are encouraged to review this page regularly to stay informed about policy updates.
