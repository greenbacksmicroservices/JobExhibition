Consultancy Agreement

JobExhibition.com

This Consultancy Agreement governs the use of JobExhibition.com by recruitment consultancies and staffing agencies that register on the platform to post job opportunities, manage candidate applications, and conduct recruitment activities.

By creating an account as a consultancy on JobExhibition.com, you agree to comply with the terms and conditions outlined in this agreement.

JobExhibition.com operates as an online job marketplace connecting candidates, employers, and recruitment consultancies.

1. Consultancy Account Registration
  • Provide accurate business and contact information.
  • Use a valid company email address.
  • Maintain the confidentiality of account login credentials.
  • Keep company profile information updated.
  • JobExhibition.com reserves the right to verify consultancy accounts before granting full access.
2. Authorized Use of the Platform
  • Post job opportunities on behalf of employers or clients.
  • Search and review candidate profiles.
  • Contact candidates regarding employment opportunities.
  • Manage job applications and interview processes.
  • All activities must be related to legitimate recruitment services.
3. Job Posting Responsibilities
  • All job postings must be genuine employment opportunities.
  • Job postings must be authorized by the employer or client.
  • Job descriptions must be accurate and clearly explained.
  • Fake or misleading job advertisements are prohibited.
  • Duplicate or spam job postings are not allowed.
  • Jobs requiring candidates to pay any fee are strictly prohibited.
4. Candidate Data Usage
  • Candidate data must be used only for recruitment purposes.
  • Candidate data must not be shared with unauthorized third parties.
  • Candidate data must not be used for marketing or promotional purposes.
  • All candidate information must be handled with confidentiality.
5. Subscription and Platform Services
  • Consultancies may access features through subscription plans.
  • Plans may include job posting limits, resume viewing and downloading features, and candidate communication tools.
  • Interview management features may be included.
  • Subscription pricing and features may change periodically.
  • Payments are generally non-refundable unless specified otherwise.
6. Communication and Conduct
  • Consultancies must maintain professional conduct with candidates.
  • Harassment of candidates is strictly prohibited.
  • Misrepresentation of job opportunities is not allowed.
  • Spam messages must not be sent to candidates.
  • Consultancies must not request payments from candidates.
7. Business Verification
  • JobExhibition.com may request business registration certificates.
  • GST registration may be required if applicable.
  • Official business contact information may be verified.
  • Verified consultancies may receive trust badges or platform visibility.
8. Prohibited Activities
  • Charging candidates for job placement.
  • Posting fake or misleading job listings.
  • Misusing candidate data.
  • Attempting unauthorized access to platform data.
  • Using automated tools to collect candidate information.
9. Suspension or Termination
  • Accounts violating platform rules may be suspended.
  • Fraudulent activities may result in termination.
  • Repeated complaints from candidates may lead to restrictions.
  • The platform may remove job listings without prior notice.
10. Limitation of Liability
  • JobExhibition.com acts only as a technology platform.
  • The platform does not guarantee candidate suitability.
  • The platform does not guarantee hiring success.
  • Consultancies are responsible for their own recruitment verification.
11. Changes to the Agreement

JobExhibition.com may update this Consultancy Agreement periodically. Consultancies should review this page regularly to stay informed about policy updates.
